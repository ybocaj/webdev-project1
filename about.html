About Boca

Career Choices

When I was little, I wanted to be a professional baseball player. When that didn't pan out, I meandered through life, working odd jobs. But I liked working on computers. I was the guy people asked to fix their PCs or write up papers, print em, etc..

So I got a job with an internet startup, which led to a tech support position.

While in college, I worked as a computer operator. Upon graduating, I got a job as a support rep with a software company.

Now being laid off twice, I realize the volatility, but I also learned the importance of developers, because some of them were the last to go. And others I knew helped struggling companies modernize their software.

Personal

On the personal side, I own 2 cats, I like to golf, ride my motorcycle, play video games, and I hope to move further South or even out of the country someday. I definitely want to travel and I've never been out of the country, so Ireland (my Nationality), Greece, and New Zealand are a few places I'd like to go to visit ancestors, stay for awhile, swim in a warm, blue ocean, and play some golf.

I've always wanted to own a Porsche and I also hope to buy a Ducati Panigale someday. Those are the materialistic things I want.

I've never been married and quite frankly I'm hard to please and I can't see spending the rest of my life with 1 person. I haven't had a lot of stability in my life due to being laid off twice in the past 20 years, so I need to take care of myself before I can take care of anyone else. So we'll see how all that plays out.
